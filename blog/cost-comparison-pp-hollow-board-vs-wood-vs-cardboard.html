Key Considerations

1. Material Selection

Choosing the right PP hollow board depends on several factors including thickness, density, color, and additive requirements. For most industrial applications, a 4-5mm sheet provides the best balance of strength and weight. For heavy-duty applications, 8-12mm sheets offer superior impact resistance.

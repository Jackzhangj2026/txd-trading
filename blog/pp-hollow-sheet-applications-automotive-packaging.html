PP Hollow Sheet Applications in Automotive Packaging

Published: May 11, 2026 | Reading time: 5 minutes

Introduction

The automotive industry demands packaging that is lightweight yet durable, capable of protecting precision components across global supply chains. PP (polypropylene) hollow sheets — also known as corrugated plastic or twinwall sheets — have emerged as a material of choice for automotive logistics, replacing traditional wood, cardboard, and metal packaging in many applications.

At TXD CO., LTD, we supply high-quality PP hollow sheets specifically engineered for automotive packaging needs. In this article, we explore the key applications, benefits, and considerations when choosing hollow sheet solutions for automotive logistics.

Why PP Hollow Sheets for Automotive?

Automotive components range from small electronic sensors to heavy transmission assemblies — each with unique packaging requirements. PP hollow sheets offer several advantages:

  • Lightweight: Up to 80% lighter than wood crates, reducing shipping costs
  • Durable: High impact resistance protects parts during transit
  • Reusable: Can be used 20+ cycles, lowering total cost of ownership
  • Moisture-resistant: Unlike cardboard, PP does not absorb water
  • Clean: Dust-free and easy to clean — ideal for sensitive components
  • Customizable: Easily cut, folded, and fabricated into custom designs

Key Automotive Applications

1. Engine & Transmission Packaging

Heavy components like engine blocks and transmissions require robust support. Heavy-duty PP hollow sheets (8-12mm thickness) fabricated into custom dunnage provide secure positioning and shock absorption during sea freight and overland transport.

2. Electronic Component Trays

Modern vehicles contain hundreds of electronic modules. Anti-static (ESD) PP hollow sheets with surface resistance of 10⁶-10⁹ Ω protect sensitive ECUs, sensors, and infotainment components from electrostatic discharge damage.

3. Spare Parts & Aftermarket Logistics

For aftermarket distribution, custom-divided PP boxes allow efficient organization of mixed parts shipments. The transparent/translucent options enable visual inventory checks without opening containers.

4. Just-In-Time (JIT) Delivery Systems

Automotive manufacturers using JIT systems benefit from collapsible PP containers that fold flat for return logistics, reducing empty return freight costs by up to 60%.

5. Export Packaging for Overseas Shipments

For manufacturers exporting vehicle parts to Europe, North America, and the Middle East, PP hollow sheets provide excellent protection against humidity, salt spray, and temperature variations encountered during ocean freight.

Material Considerations

When selecting PP hollow sheets for automotive packaging, consider:

  • Thickness: 2-5mm for lightweight parts, 6-12mm for heavy components
  • Additives: UV stabilization for outdoor storage, anti-static for electronics
  • Color: Custom colors for branding and organization
  • Certification: Ensure material meets automotive industry standards (IATF 16949)

Cost Comparison

While the initial cost of PP hollow sheet packaging may be higher than cardboard, the total cost of ownership over multiple trips is significantly lower. A typical analysis:

  • Cardboard box: $2-5 per unit, single use
  • PP hollow sheet box: $8-20 per unit, 20+ uses
  • Cost per trip (PP): $0.40-1.00 — a 60-80% savings over cardboard
